IRONMAN

Iron Man, a popular character in the Marvel Comics universe, is the superhero alter ego of billionaire industrialist Tony Stark.
Tony Stark is a genius inventor and the head of Stark Industries, a major weapons manufacturer. After being captured and critically injured during a demonstration of his company's weaponry, Stark builds a high-tech suit of armor to escape captivity and to sustain his life, as his heart was damaged.

Upon his return, he refines the suit to fight against evil as Iron Man.Iron Man is known for his technological prowess, charisma, and complex personality. His storylines often delve into themes of redemption, responsibility, and the consequences of technological advancement.
